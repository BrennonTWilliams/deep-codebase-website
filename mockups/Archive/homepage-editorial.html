Complete Dependency Mapping

Automatically traces dependency chains through static analysis—no missing imports, no broken references. Extract everything needed in one command.

Tree-sitter parsing ensures byte-accurate extraction of functions, classes, and their dependencies across the entire codebase. No manual tracing required.
